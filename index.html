About Me:

Hi! I'm Micah!

I'm an aspiring concept artist currently pursuing a degree in Art and Design: Games and Playable Media at the University of California: Santa Cruz.

I love all things Sci-Fi, and my work explores the intersection of technology and character design. I have a strong background in 2D design and illustration, and over 4 years of experience with 3D modeling/animation software like Blender, Cinema 4D, Maya and Nomad Sculpt.
